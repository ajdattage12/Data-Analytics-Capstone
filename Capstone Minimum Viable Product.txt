Project Description

Like so many, Covid-19 has directly impacted my life. My twin boys are considered high risk, the shut down of schools and communities prompted my career change and ultimately lead me to DevMountain. Lastly, my husband and I have lost loved ones to this horrible disease.
There is so much information about Covid-19. However, it can be overwhelming sifting through opinions, old or outdated information, or misrepresented data. My presentation will look objectively at the facts and dataset. 


Hypothesis
	The presence of smoking is related to increased fatal mortalities.
	Age directly correlates with increased fatal mortality.
	Being from a third world country decreases survival rates. 
	Being fully vaccinated decreases fatal mortality rates.
